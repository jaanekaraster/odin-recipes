Pesto Pasta

An easy-to-make dish from Italy.

Keep some pesto in the freezer for your next set of meals!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup garlic, lightly minced
  • 250g basil, washed
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. In a blender, add the basil, water, salt, olive oil, and garlic.
  2. Mix until it's a paste. You may need to add water or olive oil to help it mix.
  3. Add to cooked pasta and serve!
