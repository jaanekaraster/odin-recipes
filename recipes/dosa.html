Dosa | தோசை

A South Indian staple dish.

Serve with molaga podi and mint chutney!

Ingredients

  • ID dosa batter
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Open the dosa batter bag and mix in the salt and water.
  2. Heat a nonstick pan (or iron skillet) until a drop of water bounces across the surface.
  3. With a ladle, pour a cup of the batter into the center of the pan and swirl it in a circular motion outward.
  4. Wait for it to brown, then flip it to cook the other side.
  5. Remove when crispy and serve!
