Niter Kibbeh | ንጥር ቅቤ

This is a delicious base for making Ethiopian foods. It's a simple clarified butter mixed with various spices.

Add it to any dish to give it a complex taste!

Ingredients

  • 500g unsalted butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 4 cloves
  • 4 black cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp fenugreek (methi)
  • 1 tsp turmeric

Steps

  1. Saute the ginger and garlic with a bit of oil.
  2. Add the butter, being careful to cook it slowly so you don't burn the ingredients.
  3. Add 1 cup of water and stir.
  4. Put the pot in the fridge for a few hours for the mix to cool.
  5. Take the pot out of the fridge, and poke some holes in the butter. Pour out the water underneath.
  6. In a separate pan, brown the cloves, black cardamom pods, black peppercorns, cumin, and fenugreek (methi).
  7. Let the spices cool, then grind them in a blender to make a powder.
  8. Melt the butter again in the pot, and add the spices. Also add the turmeric and any other spices.
  9. Let it simmer for about an hour to let the spices infuse.
  10. Strain the butter with a cheesecloth and refrigerate.
  11. Add to any dish!
